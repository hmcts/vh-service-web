Please answer this question

At the time of your hearing, will the computer be able to access the internet?

What happens if my internet isn’t reliable or isn’t working on the day?

Don’t worry. We’ll test that your computer can connect to the internet before the day of your court hearing - and on the day of the hearing. If, for any reason, the internet connection fails we will rearrange the hearing. This will not be held against you in any way.

If it's difficult for you to have a video hearing because of your internet connection, we’ll arrange a different type of hearing for you.
